Enum TRSAPrivateKeyType

Defines the various ways of representing supported RSA private keys.

EnumeratorValueDescription
EStandard

Standard type of RSA private key

This consists of the modulus (n) and decryption exponent (d).

EStandardCRT

CRT (Chinese Remainder Theorem) type of RSA private key

This consists of the the first factor (p), the second factor (q), the first factor's CRT exponent (dP), the second factor's CRT exponent (dQ), and the (first) CRT coefficient (qInv). The two factors, p and q, are the first two prime factors of the RSA modulus, n.
