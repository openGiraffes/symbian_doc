Toolbar extension

Describes the toolbar extension in Symbian Belle.

The Toolbar extension is a component that can be opened from a Toolbar button; it gives more space to application-specific commands. The Toolbar extension is opened from the toolbar with the related opening button and closed by reselecting the same button, or by tapping outside the extension.
