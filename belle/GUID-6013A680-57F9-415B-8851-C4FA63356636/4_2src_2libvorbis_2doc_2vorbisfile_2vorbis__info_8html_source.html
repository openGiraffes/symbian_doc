00050 <dt><i>rate</i></dt>

00052 <dt><i>bitrate_upper</i></dt>
00053 <dd>Specifies the upper limit in a VBR bitstream.  If the value matches the bitrate_nominal and bitrate_lower parameters, the stream is fixed bitrate.  May be unset if no limit exists.</dd>
00054 <dt><i>bitrate_nominal</i></dt>
00055 <dd>Specifies the average bitrate for a VBR bitstream.  May be unset.  If the bitrate_upper and bitrate_lower parameters match, the stream is fixed bitrate.</dd>
00056 <dt><i>bitrate_lower</i></dt>
00057 <dd>Specifies the lower limit in a VBR bitstream.  If the value matches the bitrate_nominal and bitrate_upper parameters, the stream is fixed bitrate.  May be unset if no limit exists.</dd>
00058 <dt><i>bitrate_window</i></dt>
00059 <dd>Currently unset.</dd>
