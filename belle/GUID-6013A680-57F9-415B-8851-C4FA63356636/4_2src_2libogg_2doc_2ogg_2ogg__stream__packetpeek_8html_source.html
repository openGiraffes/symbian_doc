00016 <h1>ogg_stream_packetout</h1>
00017 
00018 <p><i>declared in "ogg/ogg.h";</i></p>
00019 
00020 <p>This function attempts to assemble a raw data packet and returns
00021 it without advancing decoding.</p>
00022 
00023 <p>In a typical situation, this would be called
00024 speculatively after <a
00025 href="ogg_stream_pagein.html">ogg_stream_pagein()</a> to check
00026 the packet contents before handing it off to a codec for 
00027 decompression. To advance page decoding and remove
00028 the packet from the sync structure, call 
00029 <a href="ogg_stream_packetout.html">ogg_stream_packetout()</a>.</p>
