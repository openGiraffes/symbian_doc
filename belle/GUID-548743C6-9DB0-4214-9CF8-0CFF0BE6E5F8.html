Status bar

The section describes the status bar in Symbian Belle and the components within.

The Status bar displays the status information of the current application and state, as well as general information about device status, such as signal strength and battery charge.

The Status bar contains the following items:

  • Title

  • Status indicators displayed in order of priority (maximum of three visible)

  • Editing-related indicators (for example, ABC), if the editor is active.

  • Signal and strength indicator (3G)

  • Batter indicator

  • Digital clock
